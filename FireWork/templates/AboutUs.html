Meet the Artist

Shruti is a multi-talented individual, with a background in medicine and a passion for the arts.
While her studies have focused on healing and wellness, her heart has always been drawn to
the creative and expressive world of art.



Growing up, Shruti was inspired by her mother, who was a teacher and taught various types
of art forms. Watching her mother share her knowledge and passion for the arts with others
had a profound impact on Shruti, and she knew that she wanted to follow in her mother's
footsteps and become an artist in her own right.
